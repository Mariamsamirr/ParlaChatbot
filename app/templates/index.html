Mental Health

Mental illness, also called mental health disorders, refers to a wide range of mental health conditions — disorders that affect your mood, thinking and behavior. Examples of mental illness include depression, anxiety disorders, schizophrenia, eating disorders and addictive behaviors.

Parla Chatbot is a website that provides people with mental disorders with a quick and accurate diagnosis using a robot. It also raises the awareness about the dangers of not resorting to psychological treatment when it is needed. The main goal of the project is to provide the fastest and finest way to reduce the phenomenon of depression and suicide that is recently faced by our society by using science and technology.
